This project is a WIP student team game!

Eidetic Eye is a narrative based detective game; you play as Charles Brody, a journalist with an eidetic memory in 1930s Brooklyn. You must re-visit the scene of your sister's tragic death, and search your memories of that night for proof of foul play.

I am currently working on Eidetic Eye as part of a student team, as one of three programmers. I have worked on various interesting mechanics, such as a first person item interaction system, a UI journal system for organizing and connecting clues, simple puzzle mechanics, and some shaders/visual effects showcased in the Gallery below.

Working within teams as a student is an invaluable experience, and has been essential for my development as a good team worker.
