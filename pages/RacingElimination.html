This was a second year college project made in the first term under a game-jam theme of "One More-Go". Myself and an 3d Artists Ethan Moorfield co-developed this mario kart like racing game which every so often eliminated last place until there was one kart left - the winner.

This is single player and uses Unity's ML-Agents for the computer players controls, the game has a catch-up system were those further back in the pack go faster than those at the front.

Althought very simple the game ended up being a lot of fun to develop and play and I am tempted to go back and expand on it with multiplayer functionality and navmesh karts instead of ML ones.
